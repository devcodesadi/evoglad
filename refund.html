Refund Policy

Fair and transparent refund policy for our AI automation platform and services.

Refund Policy Overview

Our Commitment

We stand behind our AI-powered automation platform and are committed to providing exceptional service. This policy outlines when refunds are available and the process for requesting them.

Refund Window: All refund requests must be made within 30 days of your initial purchase date. Requests made after this period will not be eligible for refunds.

When Refunds Are Available

We will issue refunds in the following situations:

Application Not as Described

If our automation platform is materially different from its description or preview, a full refund will be issued.

Platform Doesn't Work as Expected

If our automation features don't work as intended and cannot be easily fixed, we will provide a refund.

Security Vulnerabilities

If our platform contains unfixable security vulnerabilities, a refund will be issued immediately.

Support Not Provided

If we promise platform support and fail to provide it as per our support policy, refunds will be issued to affected customers.

Our Resolution Process

When issues arise, we first attempt to resolve them through updates and fixes. If the problem cannot be resolved within a reasonable timeframe, we will proceed with a full refund.

When Refunds Are Not Available

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

Non-refundable Scenarios
  • The automation platform matches the description and works as expected
  • You change your mind after purchase
  • You are unsatisfied with current features (that work as described)
  • You lack sufficient technical expertise to use the platform
  • You request a refund as a goodwill gesture without valid reason
  • Issues arise from third-party API providers (WhatsApp, etc.)
  • The request is made more than 30 days after purchase
  • You have already received substantial value from our services
Important Note

Our automation platform integrates with third-party services like WhatsApp Business API. Issues stemming from these external providers are not grounds for refunds, as they are beyond our direct control.

Discretionary Refunds

We reserve the right to issue discretionary refunds at our sole discretion, even in cases not explicitly covered by this policy.

Immediate Access Revocation

When a discretionary refund is issued, access to our automation platform and all support services will be revoked immediately. This action is final and cannot be reversed.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Open a support ticket through our official support system
Step 2: Provide your purchase details and reason for the refund request
Step 3: Our team will review your request within 2-3 business days
Step 4: If approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
Processing Time

Approved refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days. The actual time for funds to appear in your account may vary depending on your payment method and financial institution.

Need Help or Have Questions?

Before requesting a refund, consider reaching out to our support team. We're here to help you get the most out of our automation platform.
